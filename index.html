Good for You, Great for Atlanta

Atlanta Harvest will be the city’s leading source of 100% organic, ultra-local greens and lettuce. Due to Atlanta Harvest’s incredible proximity to market, our produces will often go from farm to table within the same day. This provides our customers with the freshest vegetables possible, at prices competitive with industrial farms. Our vegetables are grown in Certified Organic compost, using only natural fertilizers and nutrients, ensuring our customers receive the most natural, and freshest, produce available.

FAQ

When will vegetables be available for purchase?
You can pre-order vegetables through our Bitstarter right now - just click the "Pre-Order" button above. However, we plan to build our first farm in September 2013, and vegetables should be available within three months, at the start of 2014.
Can I pre-order vegetables if I don't live in Atlanta?
Not yet - we may come up with a shipping scheme to involve stakeholders across the US, but at its current stage, we only have delivery capabilities for local backers.
What crops will you be growing?
At our first farm, we will grow romaine lettuce, spring greens, and other leafy vegetables. As our network grows, we hope to diversify to better meet the needs of various customers and markets.
